GHUB Token Documentation

GHUB Token Overview

GHUB tokens are the native digital currency of the Gandi-Hub platform, built on the Avalanche blockchain. These tokens serve as rewards for academic achievements, sports performance, and community engagement.

Secure & Transparent

Token Categories

Academic Tokens

  • Earned through academic achievements
  • 5-15 tokens for completing assignments
  • 10-30 tokens for high grades

Sports Tokens

  • Earned through training and performance
  • 10-20 tokens for training sessions
  • 15-40 tokens for performance milestones

Community Tokens

  • Earned through community engagement
  • 5-15 tokens for event participation
  • 10-25 tokens for leadership roles

Token Utility

  • Access to exclusive training resources
  • Priority registration for events
  • Rewards redemption for sports equipment
  • Community voting rights

Blockchain Technology

GHUB tokens are powered by the Avalanche blockchain, ensuring:

  • Fast and secure transactions
  • Transparent token distribution
  • Immutable record of achievements
  • Low transaction costs

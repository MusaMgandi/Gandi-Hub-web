About Kenya Rugby

Kenya Rugby History

Rugby in Kenya has a rich history dating back to the early 1900s. The Kenya Rugby Football Union (KRFU) was founded in 1970 and has been instrumental in developing the sport across the country. Today, Kenya is known globally for its impressive performances in Rugby Sevens competitions.

Major Competitions

Kenya Cup

The premier rugby union club competition in Kenya, featuring top teams like KCB Rugby Club, Kabras Sugar, and Impala Saracens.

Championship

The second tier of Kenyan rugby, serving as a pathway for clubs to reach the Kenya Cup through promotion.

Enterprise Cup

Kenya's oldest rugby competition, established in 1930, featuring teams from across the country.

National Teams

Kenya Simbas

The Kenya national rugby union team, competing in international test matches and Rugby Africa competitions.

Kenya Sevens (Shujaa)

Kenya's national rugby sevens team, a core team in the World Rugby Sevens Series known for their exciting playing style.

Development Programs

Kenya Rugby is committed to growing the sport through various development programs:

  • Youth Rugby Initiatives
  • School Rugby Programs
  • Coach Education
  • Referee Development
  • Women's Rugby Development

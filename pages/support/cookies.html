About Our Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience and improve our services. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide a better online experience.

Essential Cookies

Required for basic site functionality. These cookies ensure the website works properly and enable core features such as security, network management, and account access.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how you use our site. These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, which pages they visit, and if they encounter error messages.

Marketing Cookies

Used to deliver relevant advertisements. These cookies track your online activity to help advertisers deliver more relevant advertising or to limit how many times you see an ad.

Functional Cookies

Enable enhanced functionality and personalization. These cookies remember your preferences and settings to provide improved features and personalized content.

Cookie Duration

Most of our cookies are session cookies that expire when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain on your device for a set period, typically between 30 days to 2 years, depending on their purpose.

Deleting Cookies

You can delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Be aware that removing cookies will reset your preferences and may affect some website functionality.

Data Protection

We take your privacy seriously. Information collected through cookies is handled according to our Privacy Policy and applicable data protection laws.
